Zona Restaurant

Zona is an Italian restaurant on Merrick Road in the Village of Massapequa Park. We first opened our doors to this neighborhood in February 2009.

Over the years we have developed a distinctive style of Italian cuisine: delicious paninis, salads, and hearty homemade soups. Chef Olga’s pasta, chicken, steak, and seafood specials round out the menu.

We are dedicated to excellent service and to exploring new ways to make every visit special.

We care about what we make

Our meals use fresh produce — from herbs and vegetables to meats. We avoid artificial ingredients and processed food.

Zona food philosophy

Italian food is rooted in recipes passed down through generations. Our dishes are rich in extra virgin olive oil, made from scratch, with plenty of fresh fruit and vegetables — because how we eat matters nutritionally, historically, culturally, and emotionally.
