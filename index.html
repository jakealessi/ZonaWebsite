Zona Restaurant

ZONA is an Italian Restaurant located on Merrick Road in the Village of Massapequa Park. It was first introduced to this beautiful neighborhood in February 2009.

Over the years, we have developed a special style of Italian cuisine, serving delicious Paninis, Salads and a variety of hearty homemade Soups. Chef Olga’s unique pasta, chicken, steak, and seafood specials complete the menu. We offer a full complement of cocktails, domestic and imported beer, and wines from around the world.

We care deeply about doing right by our guests — great food, warm service, and a meal you’ll remember for the right reasons.

We care about what we make

Our meals are made using fresh produce, from herbs and vegetables to meats. There are no artificial ingredients or processed food.

Zona Food

Italian food is rooted in a rich history of passing down recipes from generation to generation. Quite simply, because it matters—not just nutritionally but historically, culturally, and emotionally. In Italy, eating does more than sustain life: it embellishes daily existence.

Our recipes are rich in extra virgin olive oil, they are made from scratch so there are no artificial ingredients and no processed foods involved. And there are plenty of fresh fruit and vegetables involved
